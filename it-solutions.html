Overview

About IT Solutions

SRCAA’s IT Solutions division is being developed to empower academic institutions with modern, scalable, and secure technology. From digital research platforms and publication management systems to data analytics, learning management, and institutional web infrastructure, we aim to deliver end-to-end technology services tailored to the needs of educators, researchers, and students.

Our solutions will combine open standards, cloud-native architecture, and academic best practices to help institutions streamline operations, enhance research output, and expand global collaboration — all backed by SRCAA’s commitment to quality and ethical technology use.

Services

Our Service Offerings

Cloud Infrastructure

Scalable, secure cloud hosting for academic websites, repositories, and research platforms.

Research Data Management

Centralised platforms for storing, organising, and sharing research datasets with DOI integration.

Publication Management

End-to-end journal and publication management systems with peer-review workflows.

Data Analytics

Research analytics dashboards for citations, impact metrics, and institutional performance.

Learning Management

LMS implementation and integration for online courses, assessments, and student tracking.

Cybersecurity

Security audits, compliance, and protection for academic data and digital assets.
