Accreditations & Quality Standards

SRCAA holds internationally recognised accreditations and professional memberships that affirm our commitment to academic quality, ethical research, and global standards.

Overview

Our Quality Commitment

Shakti Research Centre & Academia (SRCAA) has demonstrated conformity with internationally recognised standards through its accreditation under the International Trade Council (ITC) Conformity Assessment and Recognition Framework and is registered with the Global Association of Online Trainers and Examiners (GAOTE). SRCAA is also listed with the Research Organization Registry (ROR) and holds an International Quality Certificate accredited by ITC.

Beyond accreditations, SRCAA actively strengthens its global academic presence through professional engagement and membership with organisations including the Research Council of India (RCI), the American Psychological Association (APA), the Commerce and Management Association of India (CMAOI), and endorsement of the San Francisco Declaration on Research Assessment (DORA).

DORA Statement

Statement on Responsible Research Assessment

Effective from 01 March 2026, SRCAA publicly endorses the principles of the San Francisco Declaration on Research Assessment (DORA) and affirms its commitment to responsible, transparent, and equitable evaluation of research and researchers.

SRCAA emphasizes that research should be assessed based on its quality, originality, rigor, and societal impact, rather than relying solely on journal-based metrics.

Through its policies, publications, and academic initiatives, SRCAA aims to strengthen fair research assessment systems and contribute to a more balanced and meaningful global research ecosystem.
