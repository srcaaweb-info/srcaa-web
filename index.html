About Us

Shakti Research Centre

Shakti Research Centre and Academia (SRCAA) is a digital academic and research institution established in 2024, dedicated to advancing research excellence, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, and promoting innovation through scholarly engagement, capacity building, and international academic partnerships. SRCAA serves as a dynamic platform for researchers, academicians, professionals, students, and institutions by facilitating research collaborations, publication support, academic mentoring, internship programmes, faculty development initiatives, conferences, workshops, and knowledge exchange activities. The institution promotes responsible research assessment, publication ethics, and multidisciplinary research that addresses contemporary academic and societal challenges.

SRCAA has demonstrated conformity with internationally recognized standards through its accreditation under the International Trade Council (ITC) Conformity Assessment and Recognition Framework and is registered with the Global Association of Online Trainers and Examiners (GAOTE). The institution actively strengthens its global academic presence through professional engagement with organizations such as the Research Council of India (RCI), the American Psychological Association (APA), and the Commerce and Management Association of India (CMAOI). Furthermore, SRCAA has initiated its inclusion in the Research Organization Registry (ROR) to establish a standardized global institutional identity, reinforcing its commitment to international research collaboration, academic excellence, ethical scholarship, and sustainable knowledge development.

Statement on Responsible Research Assessment

Effective from 01 March 2026, Shakti Research Centre and Academia (SRCAA) publicly endorses the principles of the San Francisco Declaration on Research Assessment (DORA) and affirms its commitment to responsible, transparent, and equitable evaluation of research and researchers.

SRCAA emphasizes that research should be assessed based on its quality, originality, rigor, and societal impact, rather than relying solely on journal-based metrics.

The institution promotes ethical research practices, academic integrity, and inclusive recognition of diverse scholarly contributions across disciplines.

Through its policies, publications, and academic initiatives, SRCAA aims to strengthen fair research assessment systems and contribute to a more balanced and meaningful global research ecosystem.
