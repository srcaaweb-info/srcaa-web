About Us

Shakti Research Centre

Established in 2024 under the Shakti Groups of Education, SRCAA is a digital academic and research institution founded by Sudhakaran T., accredited by ITC and GAOTE.

Mission

Inclusive academic environment, high integrity, global collaboration, lifelong learning.

Vision

Global leader in research & education, connecting educators and students worldwide.
